Our Story

Nothing batter cakes was founded by Kay-Leigh.
While socializing and getting together with friends and family food was always present
and nothing was sweeter than conversations and cakes.
This business came from that. Friends and love.




Our Mission

Nothing batter cakes always prepare their baked goods with love.
It’s 100% homemade. And always have others in mind.




Our Visions

Kay-Leigh wants to give everyone a piece of the joy and content
that comes with conversations with friends and a plate of freshly baked goods.
